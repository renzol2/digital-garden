# Time Domain

The **time domain** (in context of audio) is a representation of [[audio-signal|signal]]s or waves (including [[sound-waves|sound waves]]) where axes are labeled as follows:

- x-axis: time
- y-axis: amplitude (pressure, [[loudness]], etc.)
