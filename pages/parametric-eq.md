# Parametric EQ

A **parametric EQ** is an [[equalization|EQ]] with [[filter|filters]] that alters [[audio-signal|signal]] energy around a **center** [[frequency]] by a $\pm$[[decibel|dB]] amount

- features fully independent bandwidth controls
